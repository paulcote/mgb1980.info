About This Car

À propos de ce véhicule

This 1980 MG MGB Roadster is the final year of MGB production — a genuine piece of British automotive history. It was passed down to me from a family member who kept it in excellent care. The car runs and drives well, and comes with thorough documentation including an appraisal report, service records, and an aftermarket owner's manual/workshop manual combo.

The body is straight with no visible major rust. The engine bay is complete and appears original, with the Smiths heater motor, original air filter housing, and SU carburetor all in place. The interior is clean with no major damage. The black soft top is functional, and the rubber bumpers are intact. This is not a project car — it's a driver-ready classic.

The vehicle is sold as-is, without legal warranty. I am not a mechanic and am selling on behalf of what I know from the documentation and family history. Viewings by appointment only in Quebec City.
